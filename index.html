Hollow Knight Guide

Objective

Hollow Knight is a game following our protagonist, who is the child of the Pale King. The main objective of the game is to explore the once prosperous kingdom of Hallownest, now in ruins, and uncover its history. You and the protagonist start out with the same amount of limited knowledge about the world, and progress through the same. It is a metroidvania-style game.

Map

The game features several interconnected areas, each with its own unique environment, enemies, and challenges.
